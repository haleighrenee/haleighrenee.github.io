About Me

Curriculum Vitae

Hello, I am Haleigh! I was born and raised in the Kansas City, Missouri area and currently reside in Lee’s Summit–just outside the metro. I have experience teaching all levels of high school English. This is my 6th year as an educator. When I am not spending my time grading, planning, or hanging out in my classroom, I’m likely with my husband and dogs.
I married my husband, Kyle, in 2016. We have two dogs, Albus and Dobby, and they occupy most of our free time. Our favorite pastime is walking them around the neighborhood. We enjoy anything involving science-fiction or superheroes. If we aren’t doing anything already mentioned, then we’re likely out for a run or enjoying dinner on a local patio.

Outside of all the excitement of teaching and being a dog mom, I am also a student. I earned my Bachelor of Arts in English from Avila University in Kansas City. After getting my first two years of teaching under my belt, I decided to return to the classroom. I earned my Master of Arts in English from Emporia State University in 2020 (right as the pandemic hit). Finally, because I learned to love virtual teaching, I enrolled in my current program at the University of Missouri. I am working toward an Education Specialist in Information Science and Learning Technologies with an emphasis in Online Education. I will graduate in December 2022.
